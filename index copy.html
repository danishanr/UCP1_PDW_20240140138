Spirited Away

Spirited Away (2001) Film by Hayao Miyazaki


This movie follows Chihiro, a ten-year-old girl who wanders into a magical world of spirits.
After her parents are transformed into pigs, she must work in a witch's bathhouse to save them
and find a way back home. It is a journey of maturity, identity, and bravery
